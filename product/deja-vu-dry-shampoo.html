Déjà Vu

Dry Shampoo

A must-have when you don’t have time for traditional shampoo! One use refreshes and revives hair, leaving it fuller and more voluminous. The mineral-based formula absorbs excess oil and odors while blending effortlessly with your natural color.

The same products you love, now with new packaging! Please note orders may ship with a mix of previous & new designs.

Perk #1 - Clean It Up
Perfect when you’re short on time or in need of a blowout boost. Unique oil-absorbing minerals revitalize day 2+ hair, so you can skip the wash but still enjoy the look and feel of freshly-washed hair.

Perk #2 - Fine & Oily Hair’s BFF
Tired of hair falling flat shortly after washing and styling? Give fine, thin, or oily hair a quick pick-me-up that boosts body, adds light texture and a clean, fresh scent.

Perk #3 - Skip the Wash, Not Your Sweat Sesh
Don’t forgo the workout to save your style! Just a few sprays of this mane-mattifier revives roots, soaks up sweat and odors, and restores volume. Toss it in your gym bag and you’re good to go.

GAME CHANGERS

Panthenol: The B vitamin locks-in moisture & shine while boosting strength & elasticity.

Zeolite: This highly absorbent mineral attracts & helps remove buildup & impurities from hair & scalp.

Shake well before use. Hold can approximately 4 - 6 inches away from dry hair and spray product directed at roots. Use your fingers to gently massage product throughout and brush through.

Isobutane, Alcohol Denat, Zeolite, Panthenol, Butylene Glycol, Propylene Glycol, PPG12-Buteth-16, PEG-12 Dimethicone, Fragrance (Parfum).
Will this Déjà Vu Dry Shampoo dry out my hair?
No need to worry. This cutting-edge formula won’t strip strands of natural moisture. It simply sops up sweat, excess sebum, dirt, and debris from the day without a drop of water. It’s great for on-the-go gals who want gorgeous-looking locks every day.
 
Will this dry shampoo leave that white cast on my dark hair?
This talc-free formula blends into roots seamlessly, while blurring slick strands. No one will ever know you hadn’t washed your hair. It’s your little secret.
 
How is this Déjà Vu Dry Shampoo different from a texturizing spray?
Déjà Vu Dry Shampoo is intended to keep roots free of oil, sweat, dirt, and grime. Although it revives and refreshes roots to bring back bounce, texture, and volume, it isn’t intended to be sprayed down the shaft of hair as a texturizing spray is. Dry shampoo is designed to clean roots, while a texturizing spray gives tresses grit and hold for tousled, piece-y styles.
 
Is this dry shampoo safe to use on my color-treated hair?
Absolutely. This Déjà Vu Dry Shampoo is specifically formulated to save your shade and keep color bursting with beauty and brilliance.
 
Will this dry shampoo replace my normal hair washes?
Only temporarily. It is advised you use this Déjà Vu Dry Shampoo on second, third, and maybe fourth day dirty hair. Once you’re nearing the end of the work week, you may want to start sudsing strands. This innovative little hair hack will only make mane magic happen for so long.
